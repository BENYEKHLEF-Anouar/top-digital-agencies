Our Story

index digital was founded in 2023 by a group of Moroccan marketers, entrepreneurs, and researchers who were tired of seeing businesses waste money on the wrong agencies.

After interviewing over 200 business owners across Casablanca, Rabat, and Tangier, we discovered a consistent pattern: most companies chose their agency based on a friend's recommendation or a slick website, without any way to verify quality, compare options, or understand pricing.

We built this platform to solve that problem. Our team now includes 6 full-time researchers, 2 editors, and a network of industry advisors who help us maintain rigorous standards.

Home Methodology

Our Evaluation Standards

How we research, evaluate, and rank digital marketing agencies in Morocco. Updated June 2024.

Overview

Our evaluation process is designed to be comprehensive, consistent, and fair. Every agency in our directory has been through the same research protocol, regardless of whether they applied for listing or were discovered by our editorial team.

The process takes 2-4 weeks per agency and involves four stages: initial screening, portfolio audit, client verification, and final scoring. Agencies that fail any stage are not listed.

1

Initial Screening

Before any deep research begins, we verify that an agency meets our minimum eligibility criteria:

  • Minimum 2 years in continuous business operation
  • Professional website with clear service descriptions
  • Valid business registration in Morocco
  • At least 5 verifiable client projects in portfolio
2

Portfolio Audit

Our editorial team conducts a detailed review of the agency's publicly available work and privately submitted case studies:

  • Quality of design and creative execution
  • Evidence of measurable results (ROI, traffic, conversions)
  • Technical competence (site speed, SEO, mobile optimization)
3

Client Verification

This is the most critical stage. We contact current and former clients directly to verify the agency's claims:

  • Email verification of reviewer identity and relationship to agency
  • LinkedIn profile confirmation of reviewer position
  • Direct phone interviews for projects over 50,000 MAD
4

Final Scoring

Agencies that pass all three stages receive a composite score based on four weighted criteria:

Verified Client Reviews 35%
Portfolio Quality 30%
Market Presence 20%
Service Breadth 15%
